ABOUT NEW LIFE HOSPITAL

Every day hundreds of people enter the gates of New Life Hospital (NLH) seeking
medical attention for themselves or their loved ones. With an undying commitment to
excellence that meets international standards, regulations and quality systems, we
make sure that the patient’s needs are met satisfactorily under a single roof. New Life
Hospital (NLH) is located in the center of the bustling city of Sialkot. Owing to this
unique location NLH has easy access from all quadrants of the city.

New Life Hospital is 100 bedded hospital that provides quality healthcare amenities at
an affordable cost to everyone. NLH is state-of-the-art multidisciplinary hospital
providing comprehensive medical and surgical care under one roof. With a singular
focus on providing high-quality and most compassionate care to each and every
patient, we have fostered a culture of innovation and sustainability. NLH is offering
comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic services to patients with cardiovascular
diseases and other diseases. Clinical facilities comprise of dedicated in-patient
cardiac wards, private rooms, ICU, NICU, CCU, HDU, 4 OT’s , 2 Cardiac OT and Cath lab.

We as a team, focus on creating positive patient journey experiences where the
patient feels safe & experiences excellent services.
